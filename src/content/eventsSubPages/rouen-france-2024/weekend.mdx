## Getting to Rouen?

### <TramFront /> By train

Over **25 trains** daily from **Paris Saint-Lazare**.

Average journey time : **1h20** for around **20** euros.

### <CarFront /> By car

**A13, A28, A29, A150** all lead you straight to Rouen from various directions.

However, driving and parking in Rouen can be tricky. With **great public transport** connections, we recommend leaving your car behind for the weekend!

### <BusFront /> By bus

From **Paris, Caen, Lille**, and many other French cities, companies like
<a href="https://www.flixbus.fr" target="_blank" rel="noreferer">Flixbux</a> and
<a href="https://www.blablacar.fr" target="_blank" rel="noreferer">Blablacar</a>
offer excellent connections to Rouen.

### <BadgeAlert /> Practical Tips

The city center is **pedestrian-friendly**. Good walking shoes are a must!

**Main attractions** are usually open between **10am and 7pm.**

Being in Normandy, always pack **a raincoat** or **umbrella!**

## Local events and festivals for the weekend

### <Music /> <a href="https://www.visiterouen.com/patrimoines/art/festivals-au-coeur-du-patrimoine/festival-rush/" target="_blank" rel="noreferer">Rush</a>

This summer kick-off music event in Rouen is a must!
Get ready to groove, dance, and dream from **June 6th to 15th**.

### <PartyPopper /> <a href="https://www.normandie-tourisme.fr/partenaires/d-day-festival-normandy/programme/" target="_blank" rel="noreferer">D-Day Festival</a>

Celebrate freedom and the anniversary of the Allied landings in Normandy from **June 1st to 16th**.
Expect parachuting, parades, concerts, historical reenactments, fireworks, exhibitions, and screenings.
