In most companies, Prometheus is the go-to standard when it comes to metrics handling. It benefits from a huge community partly thanks to the CNCF. Still, whether it's PromQL (Prometheus Query Language), the metric format, or the software, it has issues. Starting years ago, alternatives emerged promising improved performances, better scale handling and enhanced memory consumption.
We'll dive on a distributed software that is able to hold and compute Clever Cloud infrastructure metrics (>150 Million series). Forget everything you've known about Prometheus and affiliates, onboard into an enhanced way to handle hundred millions series with low-latency responses.
