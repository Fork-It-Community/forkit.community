---
title: "AI and Software Engineering: Transformation, Not Replacement"
speakers:
  - id: geert-theys
language: english
---

AI is changing the role of software engineers. Instead of replacing us, AI is pushing engineers to focus more on higher-level tasks like system architecture
and user experience design. I’ll talk about why human oversight is still essential to ensure quality and compliance, especially in sensitive areas like fintech.
Plus, I’ll touch on the tricky issues of intellectual property and how to implement AI tools strategically while keeping our data safe.
