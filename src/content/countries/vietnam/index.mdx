Vietnam is a fast-growing technology market in Asia, with Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi as major innovation centers. The government promotes digital transformation and entrepreneurship, especially in software development, AI, and e-commerce. Vietnam is recognized for its young, tech-savvy population and competitive IT talent.
