Malaysia is a dynamic center for technology and innovation in Southeast Asia. Kuala Lumpur is home to many startups and tech accelerators. The government supports digital growth through initiatives like the Malaysia Digital Economy Blueprint. The country excels in sectors such as e-commerce, fintech, and smart cities, with a diverse and skilled workforce.
