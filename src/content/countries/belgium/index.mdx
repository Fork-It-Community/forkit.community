Belgium is a leading European hub for technology and innovation, with Brussels and Ghent at the forefront. The country is known for its strong research institutions, vibrant startup scene, and support for digital transformation. Key sectors include biotech, fintech, and cybersecurity, with a highly educated and multilingual workforce.
