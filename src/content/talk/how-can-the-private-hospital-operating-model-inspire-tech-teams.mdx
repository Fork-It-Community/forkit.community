"You Should run your company like a professional sports team, not a family" Reed Hastings , co-founder Netflix .
This model has its virtue for teams with a clear definition of how to play and how to win , such as sales .
Unfortunately, in Software development , rules are constantly changing and goals need to be adapted regularly.
Who should tech teams take inpiration from ? I will explore how HealthCare operating frameworks can be transferred and adapted to solving ambiguous digital problems.
