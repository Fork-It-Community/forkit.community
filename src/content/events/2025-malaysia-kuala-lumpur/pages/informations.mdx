## Come by car

If you're driving, enter <a  href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/YymY9NKXrttixqnJ7" target="_blank"  rel="noreferrer" > Google Maps location</a>. This will take you directly to the university's parking entrance.

## Come by taxi
Use a ride-hailing app like **Grab** (widely used in Malaysia). 
Simply type **‚ÄúSunway University‚Äù** as your destination, and the driver will drop you off at the main campus entrance.

## By public transport <a href={mapImage.src} target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" className="ml-2 text-sm font-normal">(open map)</a>

1. **Take the LRT Kelana Jaya Line:** Head towards the Kelana Jaya line and alight at USJ 7 station.
2. **Transfer to the BRT Sunway Line:** At the USJ 7 station, follow the signs to transfer to the BRT Sunway Line.
3. **Alight at SunU-Monash Station:** Take the BRT to the SunU-Monash station.
4. **Walk to Sunway University:** The SunU-Monash station is directly connected to the Sunway University campus.

## By foot:

**Canopy Walk:**

<img src={canopyWalkMap.src} alt="Canopy Walk Map" className="rounded-lg" />

The University is connected to Bandar Sunway via the **Canopy Walk**, a sheltered and elevated pedestrian walkway. It offers a **safe**, **shaded**, and **eco-friendly** route linking the campus to nearby facilities such as malls, hospitals, and hotels.
