## Coming from outside Rouen

If you're coming from **Paris**, more than **25 trains** leave **Paris Saint-Lazare** every day for **Rouen Rive-Droite**.
The journey takes about **1h20** for around **20 €**.

From **Rouen Rive-Droite station**, the venue is about a **20-minute walk** along the Seine, or a quick ride away by metro and bus (see below).

## Come by bike

You can use Rouen's **self-service bikes**. Visit [Lovélo](https://lovelolibreservice.fr/) website to learn more.

You can also bring your own bike and park it near Le Village by CA. Refer to [CyclOSM](https://www.cyclosm.org/#map=17/49.44106/1.07267/cyclosm) to see the different bike racks nearby.

The right bank of the Seine has a continuous **cycle path** that links the city centre to the venue, making it one of the easiest and most enjoyable ways to get there.

## Come by public transport

**TEOR Lines** (rapid bus lines with dedicated lanes):

- T1, T2, T3 — stop "Mont-Riboudet"
- T4 — stop "Belges"

**Bus Lines**:

- 33 — stop "Hangars"
- F4 — stop "Mont-Riboudet"

**Métro Line** — stop "Joffre Mutualité"

- From there, take the 33 bus line to "Hangars"

**Calypso** (Seine shuttle):

- You can cross the Seine on foot via the bridges, or take the Calypso shuttle which crosses the Seine every 15 minutes from 7:45 am to 6:45 pm.

If you arrive at **Rouen Rive-Droite station (SNCF)**, take the Métro to "Joffre Mutualité" and then bus 33 to "Hangars".

For real-time schedules and route planning, visit the [Réseau Astuce](https://www.reseau-astuce.fr/) website.

## Come by car

Rouen is reachable from Paris, Le Havre and Caen via **A13, A28, A29, A150**.

**Parking near the venue**:

- Parking Bethencourt (free), <a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/uAHgbJb4gHgQDV827" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Bd Jean de Béthencourt, 76100 Rouen</a>
- Parking Relais du Mont-Riboudet (paid), <a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/4w7J6fzbLAsomH5d9" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Rue Micheline Ostermeyer, 76000 Rouen</a>
- Parking des Docks 76 (3h free), <a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/NWWeLY9JtrJQqoh97" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">8-12 Rue Netien, 76000 Rouen</a>

There aren't many free spots, so come early if you want to be sure to find one. Driving in Rouen city centre can be tricky — if you can, leave the car behind and let the city's great public transport do the work.

## Once you're there

Le Village By CA is on the **first floor** of the **Hangar 107**. The reception will be open from **8:00 am**, and the event will start at **8:45 am sharp**. Look for the **Fork it!** signs at the entrance — our team will welcome you and hand out your badge.
