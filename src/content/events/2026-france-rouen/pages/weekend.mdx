## Getting to Rouen?

### <LuTramFront /> By train

Over **25 trains** daily from **Paris Saint-Lazare** to **Rouen Rive-Droite**.

Average journey time: **1h20** for around **20 €**.

### <LuCarFront /> By car

**A13, A28, A29, A150** all lead you straight to Rouen from various directions.

However, driving and parking in Rouen can be tricky. With **great public transport** connections, we recommend leaving your car behind for the weekend!

### <LuBusFront /> By bus

From **Paris, Caen, Lille**, and many other French cities, companies like
<a href="https://www.flixbus.fr" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Flixbus</a> and
<a href="https://www.blablacar.fr" target="_blank" rel="noreferrer">Blablacar</a>
offer excellent connections to Rouen.

### <LuBadgeAlert /> Practical Tips

The city center is **pedestrian-friendly**. Good walking shoes are a must!

**Main attractions** are usually open between **10am and 7pm.**

Being in Normandy, always pack **a raincoat** or **umbrella!**

## Local events and festivals around the conference

### <LuMusic /> Festivals at the heart of heritage

Each summer, Rouen hosts a series of [free outdoor music and heritage festivals](https://www.visiterouen.com/patrimoines/art/festivals-au-coeur-du-patrimoine/) in its historic landmarks. Check the official programme close to the conference for the latest line-ups around early June.

### <LuPartyPopper /> D-Day Festival Normandy

Around the **June 6th anniversary** of the Allied landings, the [D-Day Festival Normandy](https://www.normandie-tourisme.fr/partenaires/d-day-festival-normandy/programme/) brings parachuting, parades, concerts, historical reenactments, fireworks, exhibitions, and screenings to the entire region. With Fork it! taking place on **June 5th**, you'll be perfectly placed to extend your trip and enjoy the festivities.
