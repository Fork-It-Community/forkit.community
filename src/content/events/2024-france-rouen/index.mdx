The Rouen 2024 conference, set at {frontmatter.location.name},
will bring together around 200 participants and a dozen speakers. This event
is designed to facilitate the sharing of concrete experiences and practical
know-how within Rouen’s diverse tech community, encompassing engineers and
C-level professionals. The goal is to foster direct and relevant exchanges
that enrich all attendees. We are also proud to offer conferences in
multiple languages, catering to our international audience and enhancing
the accessibility and inclusiveness of our discussions.
