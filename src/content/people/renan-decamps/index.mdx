Renan is a dev at BearStudio. It all started with a calculator and the day he realized you could actually run games on it ; safe to say he never really walked away from code after that. These days he tinkers across the board, mostly around web technologies. No surprise then that AI has quickly become his favorite playground : watching the gap between an idea and a working POC shrink that much really gets him going.
