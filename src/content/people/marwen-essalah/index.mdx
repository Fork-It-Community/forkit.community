Marwen Essalah, a renowned UX designer, career coach, and entrepreneur, boasts 13 years of experience, leading his own successful UX design agency, Etikks. Having worked with
high-profile clients, including Decathlon, Pirelli, Michelin, and Starz Play, as well as innovative Tunisian startups such as Study.tn, Paymee, Kamioun, Taki Academy, and
Comporoll, Marwen has cemented his position as a major influencer in the design industry.

His passion for learning and development led to the creation of Space
Academy, an online learning platform that fosters career growth and skill advancement in various fields. As the driving force behind Space Academy, Marwen is focused on
sharing his knowledge and empowering individuals to achieve their career aspirations.   Beyond his entrepreneurial endeavours, Marwen hosts the popular Grow podcast, 
exploring key themes around design and personal and professional growth.

As a seasoned career coach, he has guided numerous individuals on their path to success, helping them
make strategic decisions and enhance their professional lives.   Marwen's deep expertise, passion for UX design, and dedication to mentoring continue to positively impact businesses and individuals alike, contributing to the success of various projects and personal career trajectories.
