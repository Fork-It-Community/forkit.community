Haokai is the founder of Zinc, an AI platform for business analytics and insights discovery that helps companies turn natural-language questions into actionable intelligence.

Before building Zinc, Haokai served as APAC Strategy Lead at Facebook, where he worked on regional growth strategy, market expansion, and product initiatives across Asia-Pacific. He also spent several years leading and scaling startups across Southeast Asia, gaining hands-on experience in building products, teams, and go-to-market strategies in fast-moving markets.
